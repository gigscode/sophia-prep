Q: What is the capital of Nigeria?
A: Lagos
B: Abuja
C: Kano
D: Port Harcourt
ANSWER: B
EXPLANATION: Abuja became the capital of Nigeria in 1991, replacing Lagos.

Q: Solve for x: 2x + 5 = 15
A: 3
B: 4
C: 5
D: 6
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: 2x + 5 = 15, therefore 2x = 15 - 5 = 10, so x = 10/2 = 5

Q: What is the chemical symbol for water?
A: H2O
B: CO2
C: O2
D: N2
ANSWER: A
EXPLANATION: Water is composed of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom, hence H2O.

Q: What is the powerhouse of the cell?
A: Nucleus
B: Ribosome
C: Mitochondria
D: Chloroplast
ANSWER: C
EXPLANATION: Mitochondria are known as the powerhouse of the cell because they produce ATP, the cell's energy currency.
